This is a thrown together speaker system with surprising performance consisting of Klipsch towers from Facebook Marketplace and a free broken subwoofer modified to work with a spare car amp with a built in low-pass filter. Two TVs are mixed into the system eliminating annoying input management and allowing multiple people to use the system at the same time. It integrates into Home Assistant with two Roku powered TVs so any user can operate either TV from their phones. It also connects directly to porch speakers through the small TV so those outside can have a seamless music transition from inside to outside. Or they can overwrite it and listen through bluetooth.
