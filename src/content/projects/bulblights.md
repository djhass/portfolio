After buying Govee lights from Amazon I was disapointed with the Home Assistant integration as it had no scenes and was very delayed. Using a spare ESP8266 board, I was able to build a new controller that had a much better integration with Home Assistant. 

- esp8266 flashed with espHome
- car usb charger for 12-5v conversion for esp
