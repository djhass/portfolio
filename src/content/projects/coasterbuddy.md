CoasterBuddy was designed when I was first starting to learn how to program. I started it as a Freshman in High School when the COVID-19 pandemic was in its first few days. It was for personal use as I was and still am obsessed with riding as many different roller coasters as I can. I eventually published it and continued to improve it for years. It includes quality of life features such as using a custom built database so one can simply search for a coaster or add from ones nearby, published wait times from the most popular parks such as Six Flags or Disney, a map showing every park to ensure you've been on every coaster nearby, and options to navigate ones own list such as filtering by search or coaster stats. 

See more at [coasterbuddy.app](https://coasterbuddy.app)

- Language: Typescript/HTMl/CSS
- UI Components: Ionic
- Frontend Framework: Angular
- Native Bridge: Capacitor
